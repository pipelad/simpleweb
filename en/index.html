ABOUT US

01
GRUPO SIVAL SAS, is a company that was established, in order to advance in the exploration, assembly, exploitation-production, industrialization, transformation, loading, transportation and marketing of coal and minerals from the Republic of Colombia.

In addition, we work as facilitators in the achievement and negotiation of all types of products thanks to our extensive network of contacts at a national and international level.
02
GRUPO SIVAL SAS It is registered with the commercial registry No. 03326803 of the Bogotá Chamber of Commerce and with the license (RUCOM) granted by the Colombian Government for the commercialization and export of minerals.
